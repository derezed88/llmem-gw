Privacy Policy

Service: Samaritan Personal AI Assistant
Operator: Mark Jimenez

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

1. Overview

Samaritan is a privately operated personal AI assistant system. This privacy policy governs the use of SMS messaging delivered via this service. The sole recipient of any SMS message sent by Samaritan is the system operator and owner (Mark Jimenez). No messages are sent to any third party.

2. Information Collected

The following information is used solely to deliver SMS notifications:

  • Mobile phone number: The operator's personal mobile number, stored in the system configuration.
  • Message content: Notification text generated by the AI assistant (alerts, reminders, status updates). Content is not sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

3. How Information Is Used

Phone number and message content are used exclusively to send transactional SMS notifications from the Samaritan AI assistant to its operator. There is no marketing, promotional, or third-party content of any kind.

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party, except as required to deliver SMS via the Twilio messaging platform (a communications service provider) in accordance with Twilio's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Message logs are retained locally in the Samaritan system for operational context. No message content is stored by third parties beyond standard carrier and platform delivery records.

6. Opt-Out

The operator may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by disabling the SMS feature in the Samaritan system configuration. To opt back in, reply START.
